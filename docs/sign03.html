Food Safety — Temperature Control
Food Safety Temperatures
⚠ DANGER ZONE: 41°F – 135°F — Bacteria doubles every 20 minutes in this range.
Food / Equipment Required Temperature CalCode Reference
Cold food storage 41°F or below §114002
Hot food holding 135°F or above §114002
Raw seafood & fish 38°F or below §114004
Cooked seafood (hot hold) 135°F minimum §114002
Whole fish — cooked 145°F internal §114004
Shrimp / shellfish — cooked 145°F internal §114004
Fry oil 350°F – 375°F Best practice
Handwashing water 100°F minimum §113953
Discard ALL food held in the Danger Zone for more than 4 hours. No exceptions. Log discards immediately.
Check and log all temperatures at opening and every 4 hours during service. Use a calibrated probe thermometer.
