Allergen Notice
California AB 2117 & Federal Food Allergen Labeling Law — We are required to disclose major allergens present in our kitchen.

Our kitchen handles and prepares food with the following major allergens:

🦐 SHELLFISH
🐟 FISH
🌾 GLUTEN/WHEAT
🥚 EGGS
🥛 DAIRY
🫘 SOY
Cross-contact between allergens is possible during preparation. We cannot guarantee allergen-free meals.
Please inform our team of ANY food allergies
BEFORE placing your order.

Our staff is trained to accommodate allergy requests to the best of our ability. Thank you for your patience.
