Kitchen Policy
No Cell Phones
in the Kitchen

Personal cell phones are not permitted in the kitchen or food prep areas during your shift.

Cell phones harbor bacteria and create cross-contamination risks when handled alongside food.

Store your phone in your bag, locker, or designated area before entering the kitchen.
Exception: Managers may carry phones for operational use only — no social media or personal calls on the line.
Violation: Using a phone in the kitchen is a policy violation and may result in disciplinary action.

Breaks and personal calls must be taken in the designated break area only.
