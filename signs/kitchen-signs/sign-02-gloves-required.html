Food Safety — Glove Protocol
Gloves Required
California Retail Food Code §113961 — All employees handling ready-to-eat food must wear single-use gloves.

Change Gloves When:

→
Between different tasks or food types
→
Every 4 hours during continuous use
→
After touching face, hair, or body
→
After handling raw product
→
If torn, punctured, or contaminated
→
After using the restroom

Proper Glove Use:

1
Wash hands before putting on gloves — always
2
Select the correct size — too loose = contamination risk
3
Do not blow into gloves or roll them on surfaces
4
Remove by peeling from wrist — avoid touching the outside
5
Dispose immediately — gloves are single-use only
⚠ Gloves do NOT replace handwashing. Wash hands before every new pair.
